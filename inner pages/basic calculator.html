Project Description :

This mobile application, developed using Flutter and Dart, offers a simple yet effective tool for performing basic mathematical calculations. Designed with ease of use in mind, the app allows users to quickly solve arithmetic problems such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. By leveraging the flexibility of Flutter for cross-platform development and the efficiency of Dart for fast execution, the app ensures smooth performance across both Android and iOS devices. This project showcases a solid understanding of mobile app development while delivering a practical solution for everyday calculations.
