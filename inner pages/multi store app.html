Project Description :

The Multi Store Application is an innovative, Flutter-based solution designed to elevate the shopping experience for both customers and suppliers. Launched in November 2023, this personal project integrates a seamless shopping platform with advanced features, tailored to meet the needs of diverse users. For customers, the app offers a smooth and engaging interface where they can effortlessly browse through products, add items to their wishlist or cart, and securely complete transactions with a variety of payment methods including Cash on Delivery, and Card payment. Customers can also manage their profiles, track past orders, and receive personalized shopping experiences. On the supplier side, the application empowers sellers with a robust set of tools to manage their store effectively. Suppliers can access in-depth analytics and statistics to monitor their sales, view incoming orders, and explore a dynamic marketplace where they can list their products alongside other sellers. The app encourages a collaborative retail environment, while also streamlining operations for both parties. Designed with user experience in mind, the Multi Store Application blends convenience and efficiency, making it better in retail management.
