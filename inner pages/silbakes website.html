Project Description :

Silbakes is a charming local bakery based in Kochi, renowned for crafting freshly baked, customized cakes tailored to suit a variety of special occasions such as birthdays, weddings, and other celebratory events. The bakery, run by my friend’s mother, focuses on delivering a personalized touch to every order, creating cakes that align perfectly with the unique preferences and needs of its customers. As part of this project, I developed a user-friendly website for Silbakes, designed to serve as an engaging online platform that showcases their exquisite cake offerings. The website features a clean, visually appealing layout that highlights the bakery’s craftsmanship through high-quality images and detailed descriptions of their products. The primary purpose of the website is to connect with local customers in the Kochi area, enabling them to explore the variety of cake designs and flavors available, request custom orders, and easily contact the bakery for inquiries. While Silbakes does not offer home delivery, the website facilitates a seamless ordering process, ensuring that customers can conveniently communicate their specific requirements for pickup at the bakery.

This project holds special significance for me as it was a collaboration with a close friend to help bring his mother’s baking business into the digital space, making her delightful creations more accessible to the community.
